# Privacy Policy — Marque

**Effective Date:** May 8, 2025  
**Last Updated:** May 8, 2025

---

## Overview

Marque is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. This app does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, usage data, or any other information about you or your device — ever.

---

## What We Collect

**Nothing.**

Marque does not collect:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, device ID, IP address, etc.)
- Usage data or analytics
- Crash reports or diagnostics
- Location data
- Device or hardware information
- Any other data of any kind

---

## Advertising & Tracking

Marque contains no advertisements, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no third-party analytics or telemetry services of any kind.

---

## Third-Party Services

Marque does not integrate with any third-party data services, analytics platforms, social SDKs, or data brokers.

---

## TestFlight Beta Program

If you join the Marque beta program via an explicit TestFlight invitation, Apple may collect limited diagnostic and usage data through TestFlight as part of their standard beta testing infrastructure. This is governed entirely by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) and is beyond the control or access of Marque's developer. Participation in the TestFlight program is strictly opt-in and by invitation only.

---

## Data Storage

Since no data is collected, none is stored — locally beyond what iOS itself manages for the app, or remotely anywhere.

---

## Children's Privacy

Because Marque collects no data whatsoever, it is suitable for users of all ages and is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated policy will be published within the app and on its App Store listing. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this document will reflect any revision.

We will never introduce advertising, tracking, or data collection without explicit, affirmative consent from users.
